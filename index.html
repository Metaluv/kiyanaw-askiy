Our Story

Kiyanaw Askiy Construction LP began operations in 2022 as a Nation-owned and managed general contracting company. As part of the Waterhen Lake First Nation Development LP's business portfolio, we are committed to creating economic prosperity and meaningful employment opportunities for our community.

Our name "Kiyanaw Askiy" reflects our deep connection to the land and our commitment to building a sustainable future for generations to come. We bring together traditional values with modern construction expertise to deliver exceptional results.

Current Opportunities

Construction Laborer

Full-time

Entry-level position perfect for those looking to start a career in construction. No experience necessary – we provide on-the-job training.

  • Physical fitness and ability to work outdoors
  • Reliable and punctual
  • Team player with positive attitude
  • Valid driver's license an asset

Skilled Carpenter

Full-time

Experienced carpenter needed for residential and commercial projects. Must have strong knowledge of construction techniques and safety procedures.

  • 3+ years carpentry experience
  • Ability to read blueprints
  • Own basic hand tools
  • Safety certification preferred

Heavy Equipment Operator

Full-time

Operate various heavy equipment for infrastructure and construction projects. Experience with excavators, loaders, and graders required.

  • Valid equipment operator certification
  • 5+ years experience
  • Strong safety record
  • Ability to perform basic maintenance

Project Coordinator

Full-time

Support project managers in planning and executing construction projects. Ideal for those with organizational skills and construction knowledge.

  • Construction administration experience
  • Strong communication skills
  • Computer proficiency
  • Attention to detail

Apprenticeship Program

Our apprenticeship program is designed to train the next generation of skilled construction workers from within our community. We offer:

  • Paid training positions
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Opportunity to earn trade certifications
  • Career progression pathways
  • Support for continuing education
